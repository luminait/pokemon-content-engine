üè∑Ô∏è Universal Naming Conventions

For all campaigns within the Deez Collectibles automated content engine.

This document defines the file naming structure across AI, After Effects, Shotstack, and programmatic asset workflows. Following this guide ensures smooth automation, asset clarity, and efficient reuse across campaigns.

üß∞ General Format

Use lowercase only. Separate components with underscores. Avoid spaces or special characters. Always version your assets.

[prefix][subject][descriptor]_v[number].[ext]

Example:

char_giovanni_pose_angry_v1.png

env_beachday_fg_surfboardrack_v1.png

card_darkcharizard_holo_psa9_v1.png

overlay_cardshowcase_base_2024-06-21.json

scene_teamrocket_report_final_v2.mp4

üß± Scene Construction Assets (Layered)

Use env_ prefix for any layered environment or insertable element, broken down into:

  • bg = background
  • mg = midground
  • fg = foreground
  • slotX = insertion point (card, item, or character)
